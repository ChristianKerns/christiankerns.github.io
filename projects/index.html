From the Frontlines of Healthcare to Data Engineering

I am a Data Engineer with a unique perspective: I spent over ten years on the frontlines of healthcare as a Licensed Practical Nurse and Case Manager. Witnessing firsthand the real-world consequences of faulty data implementation sparked my passion for data architecture.

Today, I combine my deep operational knowledge with advanced technical skills to design robust data pipelines, enterprise data warehouses, and interactive reporting solutions. I build the systems that turn raw, complex data into actionable stories.

Core Competencies:

  • Data Warehousing & Modeling: Snowflake, Dimensional Modeling (Star Schemas), SQL
  • Data Integration & ELT: Python, dbt, Fivetran, AWS, Automated Pipelines
  • Full-Stack & API Development: JavaScript, React, TypeScript, Node.js, Flask
  • Analytics & BI: Tableau, Power BI, Measurement-Based Reporting
