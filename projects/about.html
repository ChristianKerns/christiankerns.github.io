Bridging the Gap Between Clinical Operations and Data Systems

My journey into Data Engineering didn't start in a computer lab; it started in patient care. For over Ten years, I worked as a Licensed Practical Nurse and Case Management Assistant. While managing complex workflows and strict EHR documentation standards, I witnessed the immense friction caused by disjointed data systems.

When clinical data is perfect, it directly impacts resource allocation, readmission rates, and ultimately, patient outcomes. I decided to pivot my career to fix the root of those problems.

Technical Education & Drive

I am currently pursuing my Bachelor of Science in Information Systems with a Data Engineering focus at Utah State University's Jon M. Huntsman School of Business. I pride myself on an ambitious work ethic, having maintained my status on the Dean's List while working full-time in the case management department of a leading Utah hospital.

Professional Experience

  • Intermountain Health (Case Management Assistant): Served as the operational SME for clinical data flow, supporting downstream measurement-based reporting and critical quality metrics.
  • Granite Peaks / Beaver Medical Group (LPN): Navigated fast-paced outpatient environments, ensuring strict data accuracy and 100% HIPAA compliance across multi-specialty clinical systems.
